About the work

The intent of this photo series is was to simply give the consumer more of an idea of what Balters XPA taste's like. After doing some investigation of my own over on thier website i noticed there was lack of visual discription of the flavour profile for their beers.

So naturally i grabbed myself a 6 pack, had a taste and got to work.

As i was taking the photos i thought to myself, what could i do to make it look more appealing? I made the choice to make the green of the can much brighter and sprayed a bit of water to make it look more refreshing as well as to add another layer to the photos.

With a bit of hard work and some trial and error. Here is the final product.



Conway coffee Teasers

These teaser videos were made for Conway Coffee (a local Melbourne brand) With the intent to help them promote their new Vietnamese coffee blend I kept the set design and overall feel of the video quiet simple. By doing so it allows to brand to easliy execute and recreate teaser videos similar to the two i have created.
